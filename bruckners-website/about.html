Bruckner's - a family legacy steeped in a passion for antiques and fine art. Founded by the visionary Eva Bruckner, our brand has been a cornerstone of the antique world for many years, delighting clients with rare and exceptional treasures that have been curated with unparalleled expertise and care. Today, the Bruckner family continues this legacy with the same dedication and passion that first inspired our founder. Our team of knowledgeable experts share our commitment to providing clients with the very best in fine art and furniture, and we take great pride in building lasting relationships with our customers and colleagues. As a family-owned and operated business, we understand the value of tradition, and we cherish the opportunity to share our passion for antique treasures with generations of collectors to come. So why not join us on this journey and discover the magic of Bruckner's for yourself? We invite you to experience the unmatched beauty and craftsmanship of our exquisite collections and to become a part of our family legacy.

Nestled in the heart of Vienna's picturesque Piaristengasse, and situated within the antique center of Budapest's illustrious Falk Miksa street, you will find Bruckner's - a haven of rare and exceptional antique treasures. So whether you find yourself in Vienna or Budapest, we invite you to step inside our doors and discover the magic of Bruckner's for yourself. Our knowledgeable and attentive staff are always on hand to guide you through our latest acquisitions, and to assist you in selecting the perfect piece to add to your collection or to adorn your home.

Our Viennese gallery may only be visited by appointment in advance. Let us know when you'd like to come and we'll be happy to accommodate you.
